Agentrino Knowledge Base - Sample Document
============================================

About Agentrino
---------------
Agentrino is an AI-powered travel assistant that helps users plan their trips, discover destinations, and get travel recommendations. The application uses multiple AI agents specialized in different travel domains.

Destinations
------------
1. Paris, France - The City of Light, known for the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and romantic atmosphere.
2. Tokyo, Japan - A fascinating blend of ancient temples and cutting-edge technology.
3. New York City, USA - The Big Apple, famous for Times Square, Central Park, and Broadway shows.
4. Bali, Indonesia - A tropical paradise known for its beaches, temples, and rice terraces.
5. Rome, Italy - The Eternal City, home to the Colosseum, Vatican City, and incredible Italian cuisine.

Travel Tips
------------
- Always check visa requirements before traveling internationally
- Book flights at least 2-3 months in advance for the best prices
- Use local transportation apps when visiting new cities
- Learn a few basic phrases in the local language
- Keep copies of important documents digitally stored

Currency Information
--------------------
- Euro (EUR) - France, Italy, and most of Western Europe
- Japanese Yen (JPY) - Japan
- US Dollar (USD) - United States
- Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) - Indonesia

Best Time to Visit
------------------
- Paris: April to June, September to November
- Tokyo: March to May, September to November
- New York: April to June, September to November
- Bali: April to October (dry season)
- Rome: April to June, September to October
